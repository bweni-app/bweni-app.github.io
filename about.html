About Bweni

We don't own the hostels. We connect the people who do, to the students who need them.

Bweni is a marketplace + management layer. The hostels are run by real owners and caretakers; our job is to make finding, holding, and paying for a bed simple and safe — and to give owners the tools and the audience they need to fill those beds.

Built for students who move out

Search, hold, pay, move in — under fifteen minutes. Every filter that matters: distance, price, gender, room type, amenities, dates.

Built for owners who house them

One dashboard for rooms, beds, holds, deposits, rent. Disputes get answered. Payouts arrive on schedule.

Verified, not gated

Owners list free. Verification kicks in when payouts do — ID, ownership proof, bank details. Reviews come only from confirmed-stay students.
