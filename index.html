How it works

From searching to sleeping in your new bed — under 15 minutes.

01

Search by distance

From your campus gate. Filter by gender, room type, amenities, and your budget.

02

Hold the bed

A 24-hour hold locks it. No one else can book it while you decide and bring your guardian into the loop.

03

Pay deposit

M-Pesa or card. You get a digital receipt and a booking ID the hostel can scan at check-in.

04

Move in

Show up at the hostel on the agreed date. Rate the place after you check out — only confirmed bookings can review.
