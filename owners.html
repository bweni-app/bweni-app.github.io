What you get

Everything to run a hostel — except the bedsheets.

Listing in seconds

Photos, room types, bed counts, amenities, gender served, institutions. Edit any time.

Real-time availability

Beds free up automatically when a tenant checks out. No double bookings.

Bookings dashboard

Holds, deposits, confirmed, checked-in, checked-out. One screen for the whole semester.

Hold + deposit + rent

We collect the 24-hour hold, the deposit, and recurring rent. You don't chase money.

M-Pesa + card

Students pay how they want; you receive a single payout in your bank or M-Pesa till.

Disputes desk

A real Bweni person resolves disputes within 72 hours. You don't mediate alone.

Waitlist + rollover

If your hostel fills up, students join a waitlist. 30 days before semester end, current tenants are asked to roll over.

Sojaly visitor mgmt

Premium tier comes with Sojaly visitor management built-in. Lower tier toggles it on if you want.

Commission & payouts

Flat commission per booking. No listing fees.

Listing is free. We take a flat percentage of each booking when rent clears. Payouts go to your bank or M-Pesa till on a schedule you set.

Mid-term refunds: we offer prorated refunds to students if you opt in. Optional, and switchable per-hostel.
